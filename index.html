Authetic Foods:

  • Melanzane di Parmigiana

    • Melanzane di Parmigiana, frequently referred to as parmigiana. It is an Italian dish consisting of varying layers with fried, sliced eggplant that is layered with cheese and tomato sauce. Once the assembly is complete the dish is baked.

  • Focaccia

    • Focaccia is an Italian flat-bread that contains similarities to the texture and appearance of pizza. Numerous locations refer to it as pizza bianca. It can be served in varying forms --- rectangular, round, square shape, ect. It is an olive oil rich dish, comprising of a golden and crispy outer layer. Focaccia has an airy crumb, thus containing numerous air pockets.
